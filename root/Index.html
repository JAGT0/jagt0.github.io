Creative Practice · Toronto

"A designer knows he has achieved perfection not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

I'm Jharelle Agosto, a freelancer and aspiring designer with over 5 years of experience building personal and private projects. My practice lives at the intersection of rigorous concept development and refined visual execution, looking forward to working with founders, agencies, and cultural institutions to define how they show up in the world.

I am and have always been independent, hands on with every creative and technical aspect of my work. Today I collaborate with a small network of trusted sources that have helped me grow my skillset and bring projects to life, and I'm excited to expand that network further in 2026 and beyond.
